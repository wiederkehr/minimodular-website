## Our Purpose

Inspired by our own adventures in the great outdoors and motivated by shortcomings of commercial products, we starter to make our own gear. First to safe money by making simple commodities like roll-top bags. Then, to create alternatives to existing products with a feature set that better suits our needs like a dog harness specifically designed for sighthound body types. And finally, to invent new products that fit our personal objectives and setup like a modular running vest with varying load capacities.

## Our Values

### Product Design Values

- **Ultralight**: Ultralight materials and construction methods.
- **Minimal**: Minimal aesthetics and features.
- **Modular**: Modular sizing, spacing, timing, naming systems.

### Production Design Values

- **Hand-crafted**: Individually designed, developed, tested with greatest attention to detail.
- **Small-batch**: Produced in small batches with minimal environmental footprint.
- **Local**: Materials sourced from the closest supplier adhering to our quality standards.

### Experience Design Values

- **Simplicity**: Easy to use, maintain, and repair.
- **Efficiency**: Fast to open and close, tighten and loosen, attach and detach.
- **Safety**: Reliable materials and construction methods.
