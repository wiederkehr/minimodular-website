## Our Design Values

**Experience Design Values**

- **Simplicity**: Easy to use, maintain, and repair products.
- **Efficiency**: Fast to open and close, tighten and loosen, attach and detach.
- **Safety**: Made from reliable materials and construction methods.

**Product Design Values**

- **Ultralight**: Ultralight materials and construction methods.
- **Minimal**: Minimal aesthetics and simple functionality.
- **Modular**: Modular sizing, spacing, timing, naming, pricing systems.

**Production Design Values**

- **Hand-crafted**: Individually designed, developed, and tested with greatest attention to detail.
- **Small-batch**: Produced and delivered in small batches with minimal environmental footprint.
- **Local**: Materials sourced from the closest supplier adhering to our quality standards.
